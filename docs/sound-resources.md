## üõ†Ô∏è Quick Audio Editing in Audacity

### Basic Workflow:
1. **Import** sound file
2. **Trim** to desired length (drag to select, Ctrl+T to trim)
3. **Normalize** (Effect ‚Üí Normalize ‚Üí OK)
4. **Add Reverb** (Effect ‚Üí Reverb ‚Üí Small Room preset)
5. **Fade Out** (Select last 0.1s ‚Üí Effect ‚Üí Fade Out)
6. **Export** (File ‚Üí Export ‚Üí Export as MP3)

### Useful Effects:
- **Change Pitch**: Make sitar more dissonant (Effect ‚Üí Change Pitch ‚Üí -2 semitones)
- **Amplify**: Boost quiet sounds (Effect ‚Üí Amplify)
- **Equalize**: Boost bass for dramatic sounds (Effect ‚Üí Filter Curve EQ)
